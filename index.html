The Person

Originally from Sulphur, LA, and now a resident of the great city of Austin, TX, I am excited to finally be on this long ride that will be learning Web Development. The task is daunting, for sure, and I know it will get tough at times. But I am prepared to see it through and put in the necessary effort to emerge successfully in October 2016 and be ready to contribute to a great company in ATX.

I enjoy spending time at home with my wife, Theresa, cooking, playing board games, and taking orders from our two cats: Simon and Bruce Wayne. We both love the outdoors: hiking, camping, going out on the lake, running. We both also love to travel and spent two years living and teaching English in Madrid, Spain, from where we were able to travel to many different cities and locations in Europe. I also really enjoy music, both listening at home and going to live shows, and ATX is a great place for that. I've recently started to collect vinyl and am always on the lookout for a great record (I could spend a lot of time in Waterloo!).
